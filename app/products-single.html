Aluminum Composite Panel Roofing

Product Description

Aluminium Composit Panel roofing is a very versatile decorative building material widely used in interior and exteriors of buildings. Aluminium is an energy intensive material and can be fashioned into myriad shapes in a variety of applications and lately the building industry has caught the fancy of the versatility and performance of the material.The aluminium composite panels are in 4mm and this thickness includes a varying of top and bottom aluminium sheet with polyurethane (PE) board sandwiched between them, coated with PVDF resin. Aluminium panels roofing are used due to its high durability and high strength
