Welcome to Shadetimes

Is a specialist for all your sunshade needs. Being one of the leading companies in Singapore in design and manufacturing of sunshades, we offer a wide range of products including Retractable Awning Systems, ZEN Latin Systems, Outdoor Vertical Drop Systems, Fixed Roofings, Outdoor Parasols and other related products.

 

We handle a wide range of projects from large scale installations with property developers, architects, to projects on a modest scale for smaller businesses and private home owners.

With Shadetimes, we are proud to walk with you every step of your life, providing you with shelter rain or shine.
